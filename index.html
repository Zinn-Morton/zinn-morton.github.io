Math Tutor

As a math tutor, I assisted and guided students in solving their homework problems. First, I reviewed the question with the student and made sure their understanding of the problem was correct. Then I would check their work step by step with the student and ask them to explain their thought process. After this, I was able to gain a good understanding of where their gaps in knowledge were. Once I understood this, I was able to point out where the mistake was in a personalized way in accordance to their knowledge. I would then do another short example problem with the student to make sure they understood. Additionally, I would bring to the student's attention edge cases where the approach we took may not work. As part of this work I received College Reading and Learning Association tutor certification.
